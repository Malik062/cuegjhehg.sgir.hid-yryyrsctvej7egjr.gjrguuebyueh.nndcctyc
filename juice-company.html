Our Story

Obsessed with
Real Flavor

We started ZEST in a Brooklyn kitchen, tired of "healthy" drinks that tasted like compromises. Two founders, one cold press, and a mission: make juice that actually tastes extraordinary.

Today, we're still obsessed. Every recipe is tweaked relentlessly, every farm visit is personal, and every bottle carries the same love as batch one.

  • Certified USDA Organic across all product lines
  • 1% of revenue donated to reforestation programs
  • Carbon-neutral operations since 2021
  • Zero plastic — 100% glass and compostable packaging
